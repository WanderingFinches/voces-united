May 9 - Landscaping Vocabulary

Class Notes

Today's Lesson

Today we learned about common English words and expressions used in landscaping and yard work.

Vocabulary

  • Yard
  • Mow
  • Mulch
  • Weeding
  • Shovel
  • Rake
  • Flower bed
  • Wheelbarrow

Example Dialogue

Client: Can you mow the lawn and plant flowers?
Worker: Yes, I will mow and add mulch around the flower bed.

Practice Tip

Record yourself saying the words out loud. Then describe your own yard using the new vocabulary!
