Telling Time

Learn how to tell the time in English.

  • What time is it? - It is 3:00.
  • It's half past 4 = 4:30
  • It's quarter past 2 = 2:15
  • It's quarter to 6 = 5:45
  • AM = morning / PM = afternoon & night

Days of the Week

  • Monday - Lunes
  • Tuesday - Martes
  • Wednesday - Miércoles
  • Thursday - Jueves
  • Friday - Viernes
  • Saturday - Sábado
  • Sunday - Domingo

Example: I go to work on Monday.

Months of the Year

  • January - Enero
  • February - Febrero
  • March - Marzo
  • April - Abril
  • May - Mayo
  • June - Junio
  • July - Julio
  • August - Agosto
  • September - Septiembre
  • October - Octubre
  • November - Noviembre
  • December - Diciembre

Example: My birthday is in July.

Talking About Dates

  • Today is July 8th, 2025.
  • What day is it? - It's Tuesday.
  • What is the date? - It is July 8th.

In the U.S., we write dates as Month / Day / Year.
Example: 07/08/2025

Practice Activity

Try to answer these questions:

  1. What time do you wake up?
  2. What day is your English class?
  3. What month is your birthday?
  4. What is today’s date?
