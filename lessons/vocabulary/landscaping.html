    Role-Play Practice

    Scheduling Work

    Client: Hi, can you come to look at my yard?
    Wilson: Yes, when is a good time for you?
    Client: Tomorrow morning?
    Wilson: Yes, I will be there at 9:00 AM.

    Giving an Estimate

    Client: How much to cut the grass and plant flowers?
    Wilson: Mowing the lawn is $50. Planting flowers is $100, including materials.
    Client: That sounds good. When can you start?
    Wilson: Tomorrow at 9:00 AM.

    Finishing the Job

    Client: Wow, the yard looks amazing!
    Wilson: Thank you! I will send you the invoice today.
    Client: Perfect. I’ll pay today. Can I call you again next month?
    Wilson: Yes, of course!
