Using "To Be" with Jobs

To talk about occupations, use the verb to be + profession:

  • I am a teacher.
  • She is a doctor.
  • They are engineers.

Fill In the Blanks

  1. I ___ (be) a teacher.
  2. She ___ (be) a nurse.
  3. They ___ (be) engineers.
  4. He ___ (be) a driver.
  5. We ___ (be) accountants.

Role-Plays

A: What do you do?
B: I am a doctor.
A: Are they students?
B: Yes, they are students.
A: Is she a chef?
B: No, she is a manager.
A: Who is the driver?
B: He is the new driver.
