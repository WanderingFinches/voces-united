Role-Plays: Asking for Directions

A: Excuse me, where is the post office?
B: Go straight and turn right at the pharmacy.
A: How do I get to the library?
B: Walk past the supermarket and it’s on your left.
A: Can you tell me where the park is?
B: The park is between the bank and the school.
A: Is the restaurant across from the hospital?
B: Yes, it’s right across from the main entrance.
