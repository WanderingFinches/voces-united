Episode 1: Celeste Arrives in the United States

Published July 15, 2025

Hello, dear friends! I am Princess Celeste, traveling from my kingdom in a faraway land. Today I arrived in the beautiful United States, full of excitement and curiosity. As I stepped off the ship and felt the warm breeze, I knew this adventure would teach me so much.

My first lesson: how to greet new friends and ask their names. In my kingdom, we bow and share stories, but here, simple words carry great warmth. Let’s learn together!

Greetings

  • Hello – When you meet someone.
  • Good morning – Use before noon.
  • Good afternoon – Use after noon.
  • Good evening – Use after sunset.

Practice tip: Smile and say “Hello!” to three people today.

Asking Someone’s Name

  • What is your name? – Ask someone their name.
  • My name is Celeste. – Respond with your name.
  • Nice to meet you. – Say after learning their name.

Practice tip: Ask a friend “What is your name?” and respond confidently.

Quick Quiz

1. How do you say “Good afternoon” in English?

2. What question do you ask to learn someone’s name?

3. How would you respond if someone asks, “What is your name?”
