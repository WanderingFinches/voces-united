English Pronunciation: The "TH" Sound

The "TH" sound in English is tricky for many Spanish speakers. It does not exist in Spanish, so it takes practice!

There are two types of "TH" sounds in English:

  • Voiceless TH like in think, thin, both
  • Voiced TH like in this, that, mother
